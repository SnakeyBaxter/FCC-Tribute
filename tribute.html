Barbara Steele the face of 1960's Italian horror cinema

Some Facts

  • Barbara Steele was born in Birkinhead near Liverpool on December 29th 1937, although an AIP press release stated that she was born on a ship headed for Liverpool.
  • She started out in theatre and comedy, and appeared in Alfred Hitchcock's THE 39 STEPS (1959).
  • She lived in Hollywood for two years, where she spent most of her time sitting on a beach waiting for phone calls.
  • During an actor's strike she decided to move to Italy and seek work there.
  • She is most famous for her work in Italian gothic horror cinema during the 1960's.
